About Me.


Welcome to the Lamps to be Lit family! I'm Sahana! I have spent the past few years practicing meditation and researching different modalities for mental health and well-being. After my son was born, I started to explore ways to incorporate what I had learned in my life. We started with 1-2 minute long sessions when he was three years old and now meditation is a part of his daily routine just like brushing his teeth. The tools I teach, help him to rebalance and calm himself down when he loses his temper (goodbye timeouts!). When we practice it at night, it also helps to tone down the buzz of daytime making a transition to sleep time more peaceful (yay for battle-free bedtime). Seeing a positive impact on our family's well-being motivated me to start teaching meditation to other kids. My hope is to empower every child with these tools they can use for life!
